Methodology & Margin of Error
20
21
22
23
This model evaluates political manifestos using a structured framework consisting of

1. Population Estimation

Target population are derived from the Census 2011 data, and adjusted using a growth appropriation method (+10%) to reflect the population growth

2. Estimation of Cost

Each policy is assigned a unit cost based on

1. Market benchmarks

2. Existing schemes

(All values are expressed in crores)

3. Fiscal Impact Calculation

Policies are classified by impact type

- Expense Increasee

- Revenue Increase

- Revenue Decrease

- Neutral

Annual cost is computed based on timeframe, and fiscal impact derived using a sign system which you can find in the config sheet

4. Stardardisation

All fiscal impacts are normalised using our state GDP

5. Scoring system

Policies are scored out of 5 based on

- Fiscal Sustainability

- Risk

- Bonus factors (Structural reforms, Revenue generation, Cost cutting )

This model includes estimation-based calculations and is subject to a margin of error due to limitations in time and data availability
